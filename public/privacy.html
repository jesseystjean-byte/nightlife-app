Privacy Policy

5to9, operated by RoseHouse LLC · Last updated June 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RoseHouse LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the 5to9 mobile application ("the App"). The App helps you discover nightlife events, bars, and experiences near you. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

The short version

5to9 does not require an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, or phone number. We do use your device's location to show you nearby events, and we work with advertising and analytics partners that use device identifiers to measure usage and deliver relevant ads.

Information we collect

  • Location data — With your permission, we collect your precise and/or coarse location to find and recommend events near you. You can turn this off at any time in your device settings.
  • Device identifiers — We and our partners may collect a device identifier (such as your device's advertising identifier) to support advertising and basic app functionality.
  • Usage data — We collect information about how you interact with the App, such as which events you view, taps, and sessions (product interaction data).
  • Advertising data — We and our advertising partners may collect data used to deliver and measure advertisements.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, physical address, contacts, photos, payments, browsing or search history from other apps, health or body data, or audio recordings.

How we use information

  • To show you relevant nearby events and personalize recommendations.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the App and its features.
  • To measure app usage and analytics.
  • To deliver and measure advertising, including personalized advertising.

Advertising and tracking

The App uses third-party advertising and analytics services that may use your device's advertising identifier and usage data to provide measurement services and serve personalized ads. On Apple devices, we ask for your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before tracking you across apps and websites owned by other companies. You can decline, and you can change your choice at any time in Settings › Privacy & Security › Tracking.

How information is shared

We share the categories of data described above with our advertising and analytics partners who process it on our behalf or for their own measurement and advertising purposes. We do not sell information that directly identifies you, because we do not collect it. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of others.

Your choices

  • Location: Turn location access on or off in your device settings at any time. The App still works without it, though nearby recommendations may be limited.
  • Tracking & ads: Decline App Tracking Transparency, or reset/limit your advertising identifier in your device settings.

Data retention

We retain usage and advertising data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, after which it is deleted or aggregated. Our partners retain data according to their own policies.

Children's privacy

5to9 is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 17. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we handle. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
